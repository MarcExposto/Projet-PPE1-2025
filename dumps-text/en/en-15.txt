Lockdown Procedures

   Page Content

   Lockdown is a temporary sheltering technique used to protect employees
   from a security threat outside the facility.
     * Stay calm and follow the instructions of law enforcement if
       provided.
     * Lock doors and close window shades.
     * Stay away from doors and windows.
     * Move to an area that will provide cover.
     * Individuals will not be allowed to enter or leave the facility.
     * Remain in the secured area until the lockdown is lifted.
